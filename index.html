Start with the 10-minute pass. Walk the house with a flashlight, a rag, and a little patience. You are not trying to become a repairman. You are looking for small warnings before they turn into bigger bills.

Open the PDF guide

First places to check:

  1. Under every sink for dampness.
  2. Cabinet floors for soft spots.
  3. Toilet bases for movement.
  4. Window corners after rain.
  5. Exterior door seals with the dollar-bill test.
  6. Dryer lint path and outside vent.
  7. Fridge coils and back wall dust.
  8. Washer hose bulges or drips.
  9. Water heater area for rust trails.
  10. Basement corners for repeat dampness.
  11. Attic hatch for warm air leaks.
  12. Garage door weatherstrip gaps.

If the same warning shows up twice, take a photo, write the date, and watch the pattern. Good notes save guessing later.
